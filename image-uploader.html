How to Use Images in Your Landing Page

To use an uploaded image in your landing page, copy the image path and update your HTML or CSS.

For normal images:

<img src="images/hero/your-image.jpg" alt="Description">

For background images in CSS:

background-image: url('images/hero/your-image.jpg');
